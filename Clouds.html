Cloud Cover

This plot shows us the % of cloud cover in each city at the time the current weather data was pulled. The data seems to be stratefied, with large concentrations of points around the 0%, 20%, 40%, 75%, and 90% markers. This may be due to how cloud cover is 'classified' or bucketed. Because of this bucketing, it's hard to get a true picture of trends on cloud cover.

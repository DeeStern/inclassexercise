Max Temperature

This plot shows that max temperature on April 5 2019 is higher in cities closer to the equator. Inversely, max temperature generally falls the further away a city is from the equator. The spring conditions in the furthest observed northern cities (btwn latitudes 40 and 60+ degrees) are much lower than the fall conditions in the furthest observed southern cities (btwn latitudes -40 and -60 degrees).
